### Full Description (Play Store / App Store)
```
Riomedica Healthcare is the official B2B detailing and franchise partner 
management platform for medical representatives, franchise distributors, 
and healthcare professionals.

KEY FEATURES:

📋 PRODUCT CATALOG DETAILING
• Browse the complete Riomedica medicine catalog with full-screen visual aids
• View product compositions, indications, dosage, and MRP pricing
• Fullscreen presentation mode with drawing annotation tools for doctor meetings

🛒 B2B ORDERING SYSTEM
• Add medicines to cart and specify exact quantities (manual typing supported)
• Generate professional PDF order receipts instantly
• Send order summaries via WhatsApp to franchise partners
• Submit orders directly to admin/franchise with complete tracking

👩‍⚕️ DOCTOR VISIT MANAGEMENT
• Log and track doctor visits with specialty, location, and product details
• Doctor-wise order placement for medical representatives
• Territory coverage maps and field operation dashboards

🤖 AI ASSISTANT (RIOBOT)
• Multilingual AI assistant supporting English, Hindi, Tamil, and Marathi
• Voice-to-text and text-to-speech for hands-free product queries
• Real-time streaming responses powered by Gemini AI

👥 FRANCHISE TEAM MANAGEMENT
• Create and manage medical representative (MR) login credentials
• Track MR orders and doctor visit logs in real-time
• Territory zone management and coverage analytics

🔐 SECURE AUTHENTICATION
• OTP-based mobile number verification during registration
• Email-based password recovery system
• Role-based access (Admin, Franchise Partner, Medical Rep)

📊 ADMIN PORTAL
• Complete product catalog CRUD management
• User registration approval and credential management
• B2B order tracking and status management
• Custom branding, homepage banners, and AI configuration

Built for the Indian pharmaceutical B2B market by Riomedica Healthcare.
```
